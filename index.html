What Do We Do?

We develop a dashboard that monitors cleanliness and green practices in post offices using AI technology, enabling oversight, alerting deviations, and guiding necessary improvements for compliance.

  • AI-Powered Monitoring

    Utilizing advanced image processing technology to track cleanliness and green practices effectively.

  • Accessibility

    Ensuring the dashboard is accessible from the Divisional Office for real-time surveillance and oversight.

  • Alert System

    Implementing a system to trigger alerts for deviations from Swachhta and Green Growth standards in society by considering certain parameters.

  • Guidance and Intervention

    Facilitating on-ground interventions to help post offices make necessary adjustments to meet set expectations.
